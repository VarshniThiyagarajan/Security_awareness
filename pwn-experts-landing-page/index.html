About The PWN Experts India

We are a community dedicated to creating awareness about application security, empowering developers to adopt secure coding practices, and reducing vulnerabilities in the digital world.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between developers and cybersecurity by providing comprehensive resources, workshops, and mentorship programs. We believe that security should be an integral part of every developer's skill set, enabling them to create resilient and trustworthy software.

We partner with leading industry experts, educational institutions, and organizations to deliver impactful training sessions and awareness campaigns. Together, we aim to reduce cyber risks and make the internet a safer place for everyone.
